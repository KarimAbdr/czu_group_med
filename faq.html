Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Our clinic is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.

You can book an appointment by calling us at +420 111 111 111, using our online booking system, or visiting us in person. Online bookings are available 24/7 through our website.

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please contact our billing department or check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage details.

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records. Arriving 15 minutes early helps complete necessary paperwork.

Standard consultations usually last 20-30 minutes. Specialized appointments may require more time. We'll inform you of the expected duration when scheduling.

Yes, you can request a specific doctor when booking your appointment. We'll do our best to accommodate your preference based on availability.

Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. This allows us to offer the slot to other patients waiting for care.

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for many non-emergency conditions. Contact us to determine if telemedicine is appropriate for your needs.

Free parking is available in our facility parking lot. Additional street parking is also available nearby. Accessible parking spaces are located near the main entrance.
