Privacy & Data Protection

Your trust and privacy are our highest priorities

üîí Your Data is Safe With Us

We implement the highest standards of data security and privacy protection to safeguard your personal health information.

ü§ù Our Commitment to You

At MediCare Plus, we understand that your medical information is deeply personal and confidential. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your health data at all times.

We promise to:

  • Keep all your medical records and personal information strictly confidential
  • Use advanced encryption and security technologies to protect your data
  • Only share your information with your explicit consent or when required by law
  • Provide you with full control over your personal health information
  • Comply with all relevant data protection regulations including GDPR
  • Train our staff regularly on privacy and confidentiality protocols

üõ°Ô∏è How We Protect Your Data

Technical Security:

  • 256-bit Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Medical records stored on HIPAA-compliant secure servers
  • Firewall Protection: Advanced firewalls prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular Security Audits: Third-party security assessments performed quarterly
  • Automatic Backups: Daily encrypted backups with disaster recovery protocols
  • Multi-factor Authentication: Required for all staff accessing patient data

Physical Security:

  • Access Control: Restricted access to medical record rooms and data centers
  • Surveillance Systems: 24/7 CCTV monitoring of sensitive areas
  • Secure Document Disposal: All paper records are shredded confidentially
  • Visitor Management: All visitors must sign in and be escorted

Administrative Security:

  • Privacy Training: Mandatory annual privacy and security training for all staff
  • Access Logs: All data access is logged and monitored
  • Privacy Officer: Dedicated Data Protection Officer oversees compliance
  • Incident Response Plan: Procedures in place for any security breaches

‚öñÔ∏è Your Privacy Rights

Under data protection law, you have important rights regarding your personal health information:

Right to Access

Request copies of your medical records and health information at any time

Right to Correction

Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete health information

Right to Restriction

Limit how we use your data for certain purposes

Right to Portability

Receive your health data in a portable electronic format

Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

üìß Exercise Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@medicareplus.com or call +420 111 111 111

üìù Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, date of birth, contact details
  • National ID or passport number
  • Insurance information
  • Emergency contact information

Medical Information:

  • Medical history and current conditions
  • Medications and allergies
  • Test results and diagnostic reports
  • Treatment plans and doctor's notes
  • Vaccination records

Why We Collect This Information:

  • Patient Care: To provide quality medical treatment and care
  • Safety: To ensure your safety and prevent medical errors
  • Continuity: To maintain consistent care across different healthcare providers
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with healthcare regulations
  • Billing: To process insurance claims and payments

üîç How We Use Your Information

We ONLY use your information for:

  • Medical Treatment: Providing healthcare services to you
  • Healthcare Operations: Improving quality of care and facility operations
  • Payment Processing: Billing and insurance claim processing
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Emergency Situations: Protecting your health in urgent situations
‚ùå We NEVER:
  • Sell your personal or medical information to third parties
  • Share your data with marketers or advertisers
  • Use your information for purposes unrelated to your healthcare
  • Disclose your information without your consent (except as legally required)

ü§ê When We Share Your Information

We maintain strict confidentiality. Your information may only be shared:

With Your Consent:

  • When you authorize us to share with other healthcare providers
  • When you request medical records be sent elsewhere
  • When you explicitly consent to information sharing

For Treatment Purposes:

  • With specialists and healthcare professionals involved in your care
  • With laboratories and diagnostic facilities for testing
  • With pharmacies to fill your prescriptions

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To report certain diseases to public health authorities
  • To comply with health and safety regulations

Emergency Situations:

  • To protect your vital interests when you cannot consent
  • To emergency responders in life-threatening situations

Important: All third parties we share information with are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and must comply with applicable data protection laws.

üìÖ How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your medical records and personal information in accordance with legal requirements:

  • Adult Medical Records: Minimum 10 years after last treatment
  • Pediatric Records: Until patient turns 25 years old or 10 years after last treatment, whichever is longer
  • Diagnostic Images: Minimum 5 years
  • Billing Records: 7 years as required by tax law

After the retention period, records are securely destroyed using certified confidential destruction services.
