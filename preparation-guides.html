Preparation Guides

Everything you need to know before your visit

üìã First Visit Preparation

Required Documents:

  • Valid Photo ID - Passport, driver's license, or national ID card
  • Health Insurance Card - Current insurance information and policy number
  • Medical History Form - Available at reception or download here
  • Previous Medical Records - If available from other healthcare providers
  • List of Current Medications - Include dosages and frequency
  • Allergy Information - Complete list of known allergies
‚è∞ Arrival Time: Please arrive 15 minutes early for your first appointment to complete registration and paperwork.

What to Expect:

  1. Check-in at reception desk
  2. Complete registration forms (5-10 minutes)
  3. Brief health assessment by nurse
  4. Consultation with your doctor (20-30 minutes)
  5. Scheduling follow-up appointments if needed

üîÑ Follow-up Appointments

What to Bring:

  • Insurance Card - Always required
  • Previous Test Results - If you received any since last visit
  • Updated Medication List - Any changes in your prescriptions
  • Symptom Diary - If requested by your doctor
‚è∞ Arrival Time: Arrive 5-10 minutes early for follow-up appointments.

üî¨ Preparing for Diagnostic Tests

Blood Tests:

  • Fasting: No food or drinks (except water) for 8-12 hours before test
  • Medication: Take regular medications unless instructed otherwise
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water before the test

Imaging Services (X-Ray, CT, MRI):

  • Clothing: Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes without metal
  • Jewelry: Remove all jewelry and metal accessories
  • Pregnancy: Inform staff if you are or might be pregnant
  • Contrast Studies: No food/drink 4 hours before if contrast is used
‚è∞ Arrival Time: Arrive 15 minutes early for all diagnostic procedures.

üë®‚Äç‚öïÔ∏è Specialist Consultations

Additional Documents Needed:

  • Referral Letter - From your primary care physician (if required)
  • Previous Specialist Reports - From any other specialists you've seen
  • Relevant Test Results - X-rays, lab work, scans related to your condition
  • Treatment History - Details of previous treatments tried

Questions to Prepare:

  • Write down your symptoms and when they started
  • List questions you want to ask the specialist
  • Note any treatments that have/haven't worked
  • Bring a family member if you'd like support

‚öïÔ∏è Surgery & Procedures

Pre-Surgery Requirements:

  • Pre-operative Assessment - Complete all required tests beforehand
  • Fasting: No food/drink after midnight before surgery
  • Medications: Follow specific instructions from your surgeon
  • Transportation: Arrange someone to drive you home
  • Comfortable Clothing: Wear loose, easy-to-remove clothes
‚è∞ Arrival Time: Arrive 2 hours before scheduled surgery time.

What NOT to Bring:

  • Jewelry and valuables
  • Contact lenses (bring glasses instead)
  • Nail polish or artificial nails
  • Large amounts of cash

üí° General Tips for All Visits

  • Be On Time: Respect your appointment time to avoid delays
  • Bring Questions: Write them down so you don't forget
  • Bring a Notebook: Take notes during your appointment
  • Update Contact Info: Inform reception of any changes
  • Payment Method: Bring payment method for co-pays or fees
  • Comfort Items: Bring a book or device if you expect to wait

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Cancellation Policy

We understand that schedules change. If you need to cancel or reschedule:

  • Notice Required: Minimum 24 hours advance notice
  • How to Cancel: Call us or use online booking system
